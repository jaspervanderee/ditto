Permission Transparency

  • activeTab
    On-demand access only. Ditto can only read the page you're currently viewing — and only when you click the extension. It cannot access other tabs, history, or browsing data.
  • clipboardWrite
    To save your transcript. This permission lets Ditto copy the extracted transcript directly to your clipboard. Nothing is sent anywhere — it stays on your device.
