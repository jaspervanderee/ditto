Project Name: YouTube & Course Transcript Extractor (Brand title: Ditto)

Vision: A privacy-first, client-side browser utility that allows users to instantly copy video transcripts from YouTube and gated course platforms (Kajabi, Teachable, Circle.so, etc.) without logins or cloud data transfer.

Core Values:
Privacy: 100% local execution. No data ever leaves the browser.
Zero Friction: No accounts, no "Sign in with Google," no wait times.
Value-for-Value: Monetized via Bitcoin zaps and Nostr engagement.

Target Audience:
Active learners and note-takers.
Bitcoin/Privacy enthusiasts.
Students on gated platforms who need to export their paid content for personal study.
